⚙ How it works

RAG: smarter answers from your own data

Nexora doesn't just rely on what the LLM was trained on. It retrieves knowledge directly from your uploaded documents.

1
Upload your documents
Drop in PDFs, text files, or markdown. Nexora parses and chunks them automatically.
2
Vector embedding & indexing
Each chunk is embedded using sentence-transformers and stored in a FAISS index for lightning-fast similarity search.
3
Retrieval at query time
When you ask a question, the most relevant chunks are retrieved and injected into Gemini's context window.
4
Grounded AI response
Gemini generates a precise, document-grounded answer — streamed directly to your screen in real time.
